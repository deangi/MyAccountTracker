Privacy Policy

MyAccountTracker — Effective date: February 26, 2026

No Data Collection

MyAccountTracker does not collect, store, or share any personal information. The application has no backend server, no database, and no analytics or tracking of any kind.

Your Data Stays With You

All financial data you enter is stored exclusively in your own Google Sheets spreadsheet, in your own Google account. MyAccountTracker reads and writes to that spreadsheet on your behalf using your Google credentials. At no point does any data pass through or reside on servers operated by the developers of this application.

Google Account Access

MyAccountTracker requests permission to access Google Sheets and Google Drive solely to read and write your spreadsheet. These permissions are granted through Google's standard OAuth 2.0 flow. Your Google credentials are never seen or stored by this application.

No Cookies or Tracking

MyAccountTracker does not use cookies, web beacons, pixel tags, or any other tracking technology. No usage data, session data, or analytics are collected.

No Third-Party Sharing

Because no personal information is collected, there is nothing to share with third parties. MyAccountTracker does not sell, rent, or disclose any user data to anyone.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue in the project repository.
